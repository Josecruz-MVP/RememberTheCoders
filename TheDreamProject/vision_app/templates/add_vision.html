Creating the Vision


Creating a vision allow us to step into our power in a unique way, acknowledging what we truly desire in our lives, saying out loud that we are ready to create or receive it, and then working with the universe to bring it into our lives. Creating one is a magical, alchemical process of intention, feeling, words, and actions. If you haven’t created a vision before, perhaps now is a good time to start? And if you have made one before, I hope you’ll find some new inspiration or ideas here to incorporate as you make your next one.

Visions are an important part of a annual goal-setting process. They help set and prioritize my goals, values, and intentions. The process of creating a vision helps plant my goals and intentions in my head and sends them out to the universe. Seeing my vision board regularly—with images and words representing my goals, wishes, and values—helps remind me what I want to do, be, and have. And helps to ensure that I continue to move towards those things, both consciously and unconsciously.

  • The act of having a vision helps to identify and clarify goals, dreams, and intentions.

  • They help us to focus on values, gratitude, and lifestyle choices, becoming an effective tool for authentic alignment and change.

  • This vision application acts as visual affirmations, keeping goals and tasks active and believable in our imagination and world.
